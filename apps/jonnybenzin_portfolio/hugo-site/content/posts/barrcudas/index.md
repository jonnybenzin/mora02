Shortly after spotting a sea snail at Kampung Nipah during my coastal swim from Minang Cove to Genting, I witnessed this astonishing sight. A school of fish, which I believe were barracudas, leapt from the water, forming a perfect arc of speedy fish and splashes right before my eyes. The memory is vivid and strong, yet sometimes I wonder if it was all a hallucination.
