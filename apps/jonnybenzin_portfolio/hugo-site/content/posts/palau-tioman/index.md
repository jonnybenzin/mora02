I experienced one of the most breathtaking open water environments imaginable while swimming the coastal route from Minang Cove to Genting, spanning approximately 7.5km. The journey took me past the beautiful coral reefs and pristine tropical island vistas of Palau Tioman. Amidst a picturesque backdrop at Kampung Nipah, I encountered a solitary sea snail venturing towards the vast expanse of the open sea.
