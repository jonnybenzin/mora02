A few years ago my wife and I were fantasizing about a sort of Netflix series taking place on a space cruise. Something in between Love Boat and Star Trek. My wife came up with this nifty name: "Skytanic."

The Skytanic is a large "Club Class" space cruiser, with an artificial world inside. Passengers enjoy 8.5 sq mi of beaches, mountains, forests, cities, culture – everything a sophisticated space tourist might ask for. But there is something wrong in this perfect world… (haven't yet come that far though )
